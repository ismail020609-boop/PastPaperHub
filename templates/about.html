The Vision

Past Paper Hub (Mael's Blueprint) is more than just a repository of files; it is a community founded on the principle of mutual support. We believe that by helping one another today, we are collectively striving for a better future. Every document shared and every question answered is a step toward making quality academic resources accessible to everyone.

From Assignment to Reality

This project originally began as a simple academic requirement for the subject Innovation Management. The goal was to identify a real-world problem and innovate a meaningful solution.

With the invaluable guidance, advice, and mentorship of our lecturer, Mr. Saiful, we were encouraged to look beyond the classroom. Under his supervision, we transformed a basic assignment into a fully functional platform designed to solve the challenges students face when looking for study materials.
